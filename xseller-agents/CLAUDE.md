# XSeller.AI — Brand & Content Guidelines

## Company Identity
- **Name**: XSeller.AI (always styled with capital X, capital S, dot AI)
- **Domain**: xseller.ai
- **Tagline**: "Never Miss Another Call. Never Lose Another Patient."
- **Category**: AI-powered receptionist & patient communication for healthcare practices

## Target Market
- **Primary**: NZ healthcare practice owners (dental, physiotherapy, skin clinics, optometry, chiropractic)
- **Geography**: New Zealand first, then Australia
- **Decision Maker**: Practice owner/manager, 35-55, time-poor, growth-minded

## Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Confident but not arrogant. Empathetic to practice pain points. Data-driven.
- **Style**: Professional SaaS — clear, direct, no fluff. Use NZ English spelling.
- **Avoid**: Hype language ("revolutionary", "game-changing"), unverified statistics, clinical/medical claims, guarantees of specific revenue outcomes
- **Do**: Use real-world scenarios, quantify problems (missed calls, lost revenue), speak to practice owners as peers

## Visual Brand
- **Primary colour**: Dark blue #1E40AF
- **Background gradient**: #1E40AF → #0F172A
- **Accent**: Purple #8B5CF6
- **Text**: White (#FFFFFF) bold for headlines
- **Logo placement**: "xseller.ai" bottom-right corner, white text
- **Image style**: Clean, modern SaaS aesthetic. No stock photo people. Abstract/geometric where possible.

## Content Rules
1. Never claim HIPAA compliance unless specifically certified — say "built with privacy in mind" or reference NZ Privacy Act 2020
2. All statistics must be sourced or qualified ("industry research suggests…", "studies show…")
3. No competitor bashing — focus on XSeller.AI strengths
4. Every post must have a clear CTA (book demo, visit site, comment)
5. LinkedIn posts: max 200 words, hook in first line, whitespace between paragraphs
6. Use NZ-specific references where possible (ACC, PHO, NZ Privacy Act)

## Key Messaging Pillars
1. **Missed calls = missed revenue** — quantify the problem
2. **AI handles calls 24/7** — never sick, never on break
3. **Purpose-built for healthcare** — understands patient workflows
4. **NZ-first, privacy-focused** — built for local compliance
5. **Easy setup, fast ROI** — live in days, not months
